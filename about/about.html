Nazarbayev University (NU) (kaz. Nazarbayev University) is a higher educational institution in Nur-Sultan, opened on the initiative of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan — N. A. Nazarbayev. He started accepting the first students in September 2010. The University is designed to become a national brand of higher education in Kazakhstan, combining the advantages of the national education system and the world's best scientific and educational practice. The governing bodies of the University are the Executive Council, the Board of Trustees and the Supreme Board of Trustees. The Chairman of the Supreme Board of Trustees is the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan — Nursultan Nazarbayev.

Kazakh National Medical University named after S. D. Asfendiyarov (KazNMU) (kaz. S. Zh. Asfendiyarov atyndagy Kazakh Ulttyk medicine university (KazҰMU), English Asfendiyarov Kazakh National Medical University) is the leading medical university of the Republic of Kazakhstan. Opened in 1931.

Eurasian National University named after L.N. Named in honor of the Russian scientist, historian-ethnologist L. N. Gumilyov. ENU includes 28 scientific institutions (research institutes, laboratories, centers), 13 faculties, the Institute of Additional Education and Advanced Training, a military department, cultural and educational centers of various states.

Kazakh Agrotechnical University named after Saken Seifullin is the largest agricultural university in Central and Northern Kazakhstan, the first institution of higher education in the city of Nur-Sultan. Named after the founder of modern Kazakh literature Saken Seifullin.

KazNU is the oldest classical university of the Republic established by a Decree of the Kazakh Regional Committee (KRC) office dated November 13, 1933. One year after Kazakhstan's 1990 declaration of independence, the name was changed to Al-Farabi Kazakh State University. According to the QS World University Rankings KazNU takes 207th place in the rating of the best universities of the world.

The Suleyman Demirel University was inaugurated on December 17, 1996 by the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan - the Leader of the Nation, Nursultan Nazarbayev, and the former President of Turkey, Suleiman Demirel. Suleyman Demirel University is a private co-educational institution with the mission of changing the world for the better.

“Astana IT University is a place for smart and talented youth of the country, which was opened in September 2019 at the initiative of Elbasy Nursultan Nazarbayev.”

KAZGUU University named after M.S. Narikbaev (formerly Kazakh Humanitarian Law University) (kaz. M.S. Narikbaev atyndaǵy KAZGUU Universiteti), (eng. M. Narikbayev KAZGUU University) is a higher educational institution located in Nur-Sultan and established in 1994.

KIMEP University (formerly: Kazakhstan Institute of Management, Economics and Strategic Research) is an institution of higher education in Almaty, Kazakhstan. KIMEP is a private, non-profit university offering credit-based, North American-style bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degree curricula. Most classes are taught in English.

JSC "Kazakhstan-British Technical University" - a scientific and educational cluster that unites the university, research and development institutes; was created to ensure the interconnection of the innovation, research and educational blocks, as well as the real integration of science, education and production.

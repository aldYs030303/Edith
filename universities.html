Astana IT University

AITU is the leading center of competencies for digital transformation in Central Asia, the aim of which is to train highly qualified specialists in the field of digital economy based on interdisciplinary technologies and ensure digital transformation through training, research and successful innovation.

Building an effective training system in the field of IT-technologies and related fields of knowledge that meets the needs of the state. Integration of research and educational process in the field of ICT and related fields. Personality development of a future specialist with high social and civic responsibility. Development of corporate governance with high responsibility and competitiveness of all university employees. Formation of Smart Campus and expansion of university infrastructure.

Despite the fact that the University is new, it got several accreditation degrees:

  • Specialized accreditation – quality assessment of separate education programs, implemented by educational organization.
  • National accreditation – accreditation of an educational organization or educational program by an accreditation agency within the country.

  • Institutional accreditation is an assessment of activity of educational organization on qualitative representation of educational programs in accordance with the stated status and set by the standards of accreditated body.
  • International accreditation – accreditation of an educational program by a foreign accreditation agency.
